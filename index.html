About Doornhoek Farm

A comprehensive agricultural development project aligned with DALRRD policy objectives and National Development Plan 2030

Integrated Enterprise Model

AGRIMJERRI operates a systems-based agricultural model where each enterprise supports and strengthens the others. Our diversified approach delivers continuous monthly cash flow, reduced operating costs through internal feed production, and improved resilience to climate and market shocks.

  • Sustainable veld management practices
  • Solar-powered water infrastructure
  • Drip irrigation systems for water efficiency
  • Climate-resilient production methods

Sustainable Water Infrastructure

The farm benefits from comprehensive water resources ensuring year-round supply for all enterprises while integrating renewable energy for efficiency.

  • 13 Natural Earth Dams - Supporting livestock and ecosystem stability
  • 2 Solar-Equipped Boreholes - Reliable groundwater abstraction
  • Multiple Storage Tanks - Over 50,000 liters total capacity
  • 8 Hectares Drip Irrigation - Maximum water-use efficiency

Strategic Camp Division

Four operational camps optimized for management, sustainability, and enterprise-specific needs

Camp A

Arable & Improved Pasture

Flatter areas with Eragrostis pastures, fodder crops, and irrigated production. Access to drip irrigation and vehicle-friendly terrain.

Camp B

Beef Cattle Camp

Extensive grazing veld with water access from nearby dams. Brahman × Drakensberger cattle with rotational paddocks.

Camp C

Small Livestock Camp

Undulating veld suitable for goats and sheep. Multi-paddock rotational grazing with predator control measures.

Camp D

Aquaponics & High-Value Crops

Controlled environment production with recirculating systems close to solar-powered boreholes.
